English Homework Q&A

发表于 2020-01-11

Q1. What were the top 5 in “Smartest Companies 2017” list?

A1. Top 1st was Nvidia, 2nd was Spare X, 3rd was Amazon, 4th was 23andMe and the 5th was Alphabet, the mother company of Google.

Q2. How about the 3 internet giant of China’s (BAT) rank in the list?

A2. Tencent was the 8th, Alibaba was the 41st and Baidu was the 50th.

Q3. What does iFlytek do?

A3. It creates voice recognition software and a lot of voice-based internet applications covering education, communication, music and even artificial intelligence.

Q4. What is USTC?

A4. The University of Science and Technology of China is a national research university in Hefei, Anhui, China, under the direct leadership of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. It is a member of the C9 League, China’s equivalent of the Ivy League. It is also a Chinese Ministry of Education Class A Double First Class University. Founded in Beijing by the CAS in September 1958, it was moved to Hefei in the beginning of 1970 during the Cultural Revolution.*Source :https://cn.bing.com/

Q5. Why did the young iFlytek get so much help from big companies?

A5. At that time, artificial intelligence was not as hot as today, but some big companies that have farther consideration have had some invests in it. And the iFlytek was the best to invest, which had established a good system of AI in voice field.

Q6. After Huawei, how much funding does iFlytek get from Lenovo in 2001?

A6. 2,533 billion yuan, which was a big number for iFlytek in 2001.*Source: https://wikipedia.org

Q7. What is big-data analysis?

A7.Big data analytics is the often complex process of examining large and varied data sets, or big-data, to uncover information – such as hidden patterns, unknown correlations, market trends and customer preferences – that can help organizations make informed business decisions. *Source: https://searchbusinessanalytics.techtarget.com/
